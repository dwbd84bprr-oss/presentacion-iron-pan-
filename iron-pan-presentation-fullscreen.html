Carbon: Where & How?

Where is it found?

  • ✓ Coal deposits globally
  • ✓ Graphite reserves
  • ✓ Diamond formations
  • ✓ Major: China, India, Australia, USA, Indonesia

How is it extracted?

  1. 1. Mine coal deposits
  2. 2. Heat coal to create coke
  3. 3. Coke helps extract iron
  4. 4. Combine with iron in furnace

Iron: Where & How?

Where is it found?

  • ✓ Hematite ore rocks
  • ✓ Magnetite deposits
  • ✓ Major: Australia, Brazil
  • ✓ Also: China, India, Russia

How is it extracted?

  1. 1. Mine iron ore
  2. 2. Heat in blast furnace
  3. 3. Add coke for reaction
  4. 4. Separate iron from ore

Environmental Effects

Extraction Process

  • ⚙ Mine iron ore and coal deposits
  • ⚙ Crush ore and heat in blast furnace
  • ⚙ Heat coal without oxygen to make coke
  • ⚙ Coke removes oxygen and provides heat

Environmental Impact

  • ⚠ Mining destroys habitats and wildlife
  • ⚠ Causes soil erosion and water pollution
  • ⚠ Extraction uses significant energy
  • ⚠ Releases CO₂ contributing to climate change
